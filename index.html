About Us

Who we are

Connecticut People Records is a Record label designed to allow artist from Connecticut to have an outlet for their music to be exposed on a state and national level. Connecticut People Records is an acute label looking for Major Record Labels and Radio Stations to recognize the talent Connecticut Artist present. I am asking for feedback on Connecticut People Record Cd Demo's. I am trying to establish a connection between Connecticut People Records and other major labels in the current industry.


Yung D is an artist and president of Connecticut People Records just trying to expose himself and Real Connecticut Artist by any means. He has the talent, beats, and producing skills to put himself or others in the CT area out.
